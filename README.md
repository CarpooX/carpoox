## About Carpoox

Carpoox is a modern peer-to-peer marketplace designed to make local commerce simpler, faster, and more trustworthy.

Whether you're looking to sell unused items, discover great local deals, browse across multiple categories, or connect directly with buyers and sellers, Carpoox provides a clean and intuitive experience built around trust, simplicity, and accessibility.

Unlike traditional classified platforms, Carpoox focuses on modern design, seamless navigation, intelligent discovery, and meaningful user interactions.

The platform combines marketplace browsing, real-time communication, seller transparency, AI-powered assistance, listing promotions, and trust-focused features into a unified experience.

---

## Vision

To build a trusted local commerce ecosystem where discovering, buying, and selling products becomes effortless, transparent, and accessible for everyone.

Carpoox aims to empower communities by helping products find new owners while reducing waste and creating opportunities through local trade.
